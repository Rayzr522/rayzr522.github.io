Who am I?

I am a young freelance developer with a passion for programming. For the last 6 years of my life, I have done everything from web design to back-end development, scripting, games, and much more. Code has always been an interest of mine, and I've been fascinated with it for as long I can remember. From an early age, I taught myself my first few languages, and quickly became proficient, creating a small game back in 2013. Ever since I wrote my first "Hello, world!" program in Python, I have never stopped my quest for knowledge, constantly exploring new languages, technologies, concepts, libraries, and ideaologies.

Besides programming, I love reading, gaming, working out, parkour, food, and math. Narnia is my favorite book series, my favorite video game is Garry's Mod, I love to do crunches and pull-ups when I work out, a dash vault is the hardest parkour move I've ever done, pizza is easily my favorite food, and I'm madly in love with all things math.

What do I know?

I've done a little bit of everything, from JavaScript to Java, HTML to Ruby. I have experience in over a dozen languages, and I can learn a new one on demand in anywhere from a day to a week at most, if a project requires it.
